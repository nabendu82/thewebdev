Welcome to part-14 of the series. In this part we will start my creating a new **Content Model**, for blog in our site. So, head over to the Contentful dashboard and click on **Add content type** in **Content model**.

![Contentful](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/1_7rqLiWp-W.png)*Contentful*

In the pop-up window give a **Name** and **Description** and click on **Create**.

![Create content type](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/2_S_r4fDpOCx.png)*Create content type*

In the next screen, after you click on **Add field** you will get below pop-up. Click on **Text** in this window.

![Add new field](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/3_th4NiBLW01.png)*Add new field*

In the next window give a **Name** and then click on **Create and configure**

![title](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/4_qswhNxuGi.png)*title*

In the next window first click on **Validations** tab. After that click on the checkbox **Required field** and then **Save** button.

![Required](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/5_oPqhQXHdJs.png)*Required*

We will follow exactly same process to create a **slug** field.

![slug](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/6_i6jW8DsIf.png)*slug*

Next, we will add a **Date and time** field.

![Date](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/7_LkqyAq0eyJ.png)*Date*

Give this field a **Name** and click on **Create and configure**. You need to follow the same process as title filed to make it **required** field.

![published](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/8_UA0iwZ7dg2.png)*published*

Next, we will create a description field. It will be **Text field**, but a **Long text, full-text search**. As usual create it a required field.

![description](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/9_jiXl_strqe.png)*description*

Also, create an author filed. But don’t make it required by clicking on **Create**.

![author](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/10_8m_HDvfrM4.png)*author*

After this click on **Save** at the top right corner to save this **Content Model**.

![post content model](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/11_SGOS0RI8I.png)*post content model*

Next, let’s add some data into the **Content Model**. First goto the **Content** tab and then click on **Add entry**. After that click on **post**.

![post](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/12_RFdjqs5Fx9.png)*post*

Now, for the post i am taking real blogs from medium. We will put it in the description field, but it takes mark down file. So, we can convert the medium post to markdown by [this](https://medium.com/@macropus/export-your-medium-posts-to-markdown-b5ccc8cb0050) awesome npm package.

![Content](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/13_2sjn_fEuJH.png)*Content*

I have added four original blogs till now from medium.com. Will add more later as i am also planning to visit Hampi soon.

![Hippy Hampi blogs](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/14_wAgJdbKge.png)*Hippy Hampi blogs*

One thing which i forgot to add in the **Content model** is an image field. So, i will head over to the **Content model — post** and add an **Media** field.

![Media field](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/15_fLawKjO6um.png)*Media field*

Give it a name **image** and then click on **Create and configure** to make it a required field.

![Name](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/16_vSt1WjMxa.png)*Name*

Next, save it by clicking on **Save** button.

![Save](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/17_AkKWgM9Sw.png)*Save*

I have also added the required image in all posts. After that too get access to these new post, we need to restart the server. So, go ahead and stop **gatsby develop** from terminal and re-run it.

Also, refresh the graphql in browser and write the below query to display all posts.

![Our query](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/GatsbyTourism/14/18_-pYBzIiTA3.png)*Our query*

This completes part-14 of the series.
