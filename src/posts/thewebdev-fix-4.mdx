Welcome to part-3 of the series. We will start with creating an **iam** account before moving forward, as that is what is recommended by AWS. I will be following along with [this](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/IAM/latest/UserGuide/getting-started_create-admin-group.html) article from AWS.

First, click on your name and then My Account in aws console.

![AWS console](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/2_lKnXoPLLZ7.png)*AWS console*

After that scroll a bit down and you will find **IAM User and Role Access to Billing Information** section. Click on the **Edit** link here.

![IAM](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/3_0KErxfsdH.png)*IAM*

Then a checkbox with **Activate IAM Access** will come. Tick the checkbox and click on **Update** button.

![Activate](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/4_ANm9TZlK1P.png)*Activate*

After that click on IAM in the left menu, to go back to the IAM dashboard.

![IAM Dashboard](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/5_zwsvAiOdar.png)*IAM Dashboard*

Then, in the dashboard click on the **Users**.

![Users](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/6_MNdpFFAvDj.png)*Users*

Here, we will have no users. So, click on **Add user** button.

![Add User](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/7_pwS8stm23.png)*Add User*

Then give the **User name** as **Administrator** and click on the checkbox next to **AWS Management Console access** checkbox. The other two marked things are as per your choice. After that click on **Next: Permissions** button.

![Administrator](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/8_AVf7pwdi4Z.png)*Administrator*

Next, click on **Add user to group** and then the **Create group** button.

![Create group](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/9_awX5Uv4nkV.png)*Create group*

Now, a pop-up will come. Enter **Administrators** in the **Group name** and also click on the checkbox, next to **AdministratorAccess**. After that click on **Create group** button.

![Administrator](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/10_KHZyF10zZU.png)*Administrator*

Now, we will be taken back to the earlier page which now have, an entry for **Administrators**. Click on the **Next: Tags** button on the bottom right of the page.

![Next](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/11_OvI5t4UQX3.png)*Next*

In the next page, enter some tags and click on **Next: Review** button.

![Tags](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/12_ejXkMRMf4.png)*Tags*

In the Review page, once review everything is right and then click on **Create user** button.

![Create user](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/13_h5yZ1pT3tP.png)*Create user*

Now our IAM user with administrator access is created. Here, we can get the password and also send ourself a mail with instructions.

![Admin](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/4/14_ELYHFiSgUY.png)*Admin*

This completes part-3 of the series. See you soon.
