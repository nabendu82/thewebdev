Welcome to a brand new MEAN stack project. Here, we are going to build an employee management application from scratch, with MEAN(MongoDB, Express, Angular, NodeJS) stack.

You can also watch it on YouTube.

https://youtu.be/qB6hfyfv2Ak

We are going to install and use MongoDB locally. So, head over to [https://www.mongodb.com](https://www.mongodb.com/) and go to Products -> Community Server.

![Community Server](https://res.cloudinary.com/dxkxvfo2o/image/upload/v1661781384/New-blogs/Yashvendra8_mdx/2_iruob0.png)_Community Server_

In the next page, click on the Download link and store it on local system.

![Download](https://res.cloudinary.com/dxkxvfo2o/image/upload/v1661781384/New-blogs/Yashvendra8_mdx/3_yazkcr.png)_Download_

Most of the installation is straight forward on Windows. But, we will work with MongoDB as Network Service. So, in Service configuration, choose as below

![Server](https://res.cloudinary.com/dxkxvfo2o/image/upload/v1661781386/New-blogs/Yashvendra8_mdx/4_dvmiyf.png)_Server_

![With Compass](https://res.cloudinary.com/dxkxvfo2o/image/upload/v1661781387/New-blogs/Yashvendra8_mdx/5_lofkp8.png)_With Compass_
