Next, we will do a eCommerce shopping cart animation. Here, if we click on any it to Add to Basket, it will animate before moving to basket.

        <header>

            <li>

            <button>Add to Basket</button>
            </li>
            <li>
            <img src="img/t-3.jpg">

            <li>
            <img src="img/t-2.jpg">

                            $(".zoom").remove();
                        }, 1000);
                });
