I get a lot of visits from mobile devices in my site [https://thewebdev.tech/](https://thewebdev.tech/) and i got some issues while visiting my site on mobile.

So, finally decided to fix it. The first issue comes when the site loads, it shows the taglist component in the middle for a second.

![Site Loading](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/6/1_09fZuKlgw.gif)*Site Loading*

I did the following changes in **home.module.css** file. So, instead of display: none, i am using another trick to take the items, which needed to be hidden out of the screen.

Next, i am also making the mobile-menu sticky, so that it stays even if the user scrolls the page.

![home.module.css](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/6/2_0Hf4-R106.png)*home.module.css*

Now, i am also making the main navbar sticky on all screens, by again making it **position: sticky **in **navbar.module.css** file.

![navbar.module.css](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/6/3_GOA4PDS4M6.png)*navbar.module.css*

After this we are making the left menu in mobile of **position: fixed** and also increasing the width a bit. We are updating it in the **Menu.styled.js** file.

![Menu.styled.js](https://ik.imagekit.io/thewebdev/thewebdev-fixes/6/4_YteJ6D0yD.png)*Menu.styled.js*
